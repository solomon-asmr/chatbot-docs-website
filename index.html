A. System Description

The platform is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) based chatbot builder that lets website owners create a custom AI assistant trained on their own website content. In essence, it's like having a version of ChatGPT tailored to your site – it can instantly answer visitor questions using the website’s data as the knowledge base.

Website owners simply provide their site URL and get an embeddable chat widget for their pages.

Key Features:

  • Voice Input: Visitors can speak their questions.
  • Multilingual Interaction: Supports users asking questions in different languages.
  • Image/Screenshot Analysis: Visitors can ask questions about uploaded images or screenshots.

This ensures a user-friendly experience where, for example, non-English speakers can ask in their own language and even upload a screenshot with a question, and still receive accurate answers drawn from the site’s content.
